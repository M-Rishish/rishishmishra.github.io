About
I am a PhD scholar in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jammu, India. My research focuses on the thermodynamic and hydrodynamic mechanisms governing microswimmer motion near fluid–fluid interfaces. Using lattice Boltzmann modeling, LBM, and squirmer-based Simulations, I investigate interface crossing, trapping, and flow–structure interactions in low-Reynolds-number flows.
