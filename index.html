About Me

I am a PhD scholar in Thermal Engineering at IIT Jammu. My research focuses on the hydrodynamics of microswimmers, their interaction with fluid–fluid interfaces, and computational modeling using the squirmer framework.

Research Interests

  • Low Reynolds number flows
  • Fluid dynamics
  • Microswimmers and active matter
  • Fluid–fluid interface interactions
  • Squirmer model simulations
