About
I am a PhD scholar in Thermal Engineering at IIT Jammu. My research focuses on the thermodynamic and hydrodynamic mechanisms governing microswimmer motion near fluid–fluid interfaces. Using continuum modeling and squirmer-based simulations, I investigate interface crossing, trapping, and flow–structure interactions in low-Reynolds-number flows.
