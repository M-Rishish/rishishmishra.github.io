About
I am a PhD scholar in Thermal Engineering at IIT Jammu. My research explores the thermodynamic and hydrodynamic mechanisms governing microswimmer motion near fluid–fluid interfaces, with emphasis on interface crossing, trapping, and flow-structure interactions using continuum and squirmer-based models.
